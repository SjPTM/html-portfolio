Top 3 Favorite Movies of All Time

Last Updated December 28, 2023

The Green Mile

A tale set on death row in a Southern jail, where gentle giant John possesses the mysterious power to heal people's ailments. Written by Stephen King, and starring Tom Hanks. What's not to love?

All the Pretty Horses

A 2000 American Western film produced and directed by Billy Bob Thornton, based on Cormac McCarthy's novel of the same name, starring Matt Damon and Penelope Cruz. Nominated for Best Original Score and Best Supporting Actor, this stunningly visual film won Best Screenplay by the Nation Board of Review in 2000.

The Sixth Sense

a 1999 American psychological thriller film written and directed by M. Night Shyamalan. It stars Bruce Willis as a child psychologist whose patient (Haley Joel Osment) claims he can see and talk to the dead. Very few movies can surprise me, yet this nail biter has one of the best endings that has stuck with me through the years.
